4. Algorithms

4.1 Master Theorem

For recurrences of the form: T(n) = aT(n/b) + O(n^c)

CaseConditionResult
Case 1c < log_b(a)T(n) = O(n^(log_b a))
Case 2c = log_b(a)T(n) = O(n^c log n)
Case 3c > log_b(a)T(n) = O(n^c)
